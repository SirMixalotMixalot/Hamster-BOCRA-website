FORMATTING RULES
=================================================================

1. ALWAYS note your source after each section
   Format: "Source: [URL or document name], accessed [date]"

2. If you used GenAI to summarize, note it:
   "Summarized with ChatGPT from [source document name]"

3. Use plain language — write for regular citizens, not lawyers
   Bad: "Pursuant to Section 14(2) of the Act..."
   Good: "According to the BOCRA Act, you have the right to..."

4. If you can't find something, write:
   "[NOT FOUND — need to request from BOCRA directly]"
   Do NOT make stuff up.

5. If information seems outdated, note it:
   "[Last updated: 2023 — may need verification]"

6. Currency is BWP (Botswana Pula) — always specify

7. Phone format: +267 XXX XXXX

8. All links should be full URLs (https://...)
